Detailed Description

Calculates disturbance as both natural and human-influenced fires.

CTEM represents disturbance as both natural and human-influenced fires. The original fire parametrization corresponding to CTEM v. 1.0 is described in Arora and Boer (2005) [5]. The parametrization has since been adapted and used in several other DGVMs (Kloster et al. 2010 [48], Kloster et al. 2012 [49], Migliavacca et al. 2013 [65], and Li et al. 2012 [57]). CTEM v. 2.0 incorporates changes suggested in these studies as well as several new improvements.
